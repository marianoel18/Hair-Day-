About Us

Based in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color, and styling. We also offer makeup and nail services, priding ourselves on our warm and relaxing atmosphere. Best known for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts, we strive to create a "treat yourself" environment for our clients.

Our Services

We specialize in:

  • Haircuts
  • Coloring Techniques
  • Styling
  • Makeup
  • Nail Services

To keep you inspired between appointments, our team regularly shares makeup and nail tutorials on our website.
